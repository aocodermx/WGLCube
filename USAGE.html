Usage for WGLCube.Player

To display a WGLCube.Player plugin, you only need a div element with a className wglcube and specify the following parameters.

  • data-size is the size for the cube to display, 2 to 12 are supported until now.
  • data-init are the initialization steps separated by spaces.
  • data-steps are the steps separated by spaces to play in the cube.
  • data-show are the index for the cubes to hightlight.
Cube Size HTML Code Output
2x2x2 
<div class="wglcube_player"
    data-size="2"
    data-init="L U D"
    data-steps="D U L D R2"
    style="width:200px;height:300px">
</div>
